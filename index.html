Welcome to Our Clinic

Dr. Jay Kumar Verma

A renowned homeopathic doctor with over 16–17 years of experience in providing expert care through homeopathy.

His medical journey began with his graduation in Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (B.H.M.S.) from Pt. Jawahar Lal Nehru Homeopathic Medical College, Kanpur, affiliated with Bhim Rao University, Agra, Uttar Pradesh.

Dr. Verma's relentless pursuit of excellence led him to further studies, including his Postgraduate Homeopathy qualification (PG Hom) from Hahnemann College of Homeopathy, London, followed by the prestigious Fellowship in Homeopathy (F.H.C.H.-UK) and an MD from Tantia University.

During his training, he contributed to prevention and treatment efforts during the 2008 Chikungunya epidemic in Kanpur, under the guidance of Dr. Harsh Nigam.
